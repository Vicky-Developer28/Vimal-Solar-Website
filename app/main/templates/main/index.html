About Us

5+ Years of Expertise in Solar and Security

Vimal Solar was officially established in 2024 as a GST-registered company
(GST Number:33ARHPV013Q1ZB) and proudly partners with renowned brands like UTL Solar and Adani Solar. However, our roots trace back to 2020, where we honed our skills and built extensive expertise while working under other companies.

Today, with over five years of experience, we excel in delivering high-quality solar power solutions and advanced CCTV systems. From layout design and system planning to installation and ongoing maintenance, we manage every step with precision and dedication.
